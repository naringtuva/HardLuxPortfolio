The Characters

HARDLUX

A sentient robot designed for a war that took place lifetimes ago.
He wakes up and realizes quickly that his time has passed and that
he needs to find a new place in this world.

JAB JAB

Cares about nothing in this world more than money.
Money, money, money.
If you think he’s your friend, think again.
If there is money to be gained, he'll throw you
under the bus. No second thoughts.

HAROLD

A gifted, unorthodox detective turned insane vigilante.
Previously a lumminary for the police department with a lonely private life.
During an experiment to recreate his favourite pepper sauce
he accidentally uses a new experimental opium flower instead of regular peppers.
This turns him insane and to a path of revenge against the local cartel.

MATOUK

Son to a brilliant scientist who was at the forefront
of weapons and robotics.
His father has disappeared with no trace but
his newest invention left behind for his son.
He gave it the name Rocket Armour.
Matouk now takes to the skies to search for his lost father.

CATDUDE

A tennis ball monk.
What monastery teaches this? She won’t say.
Where is she from? She won’t say.
What is her purpose? She has no idea.
All we know is that she’s here and along for the ride.
Always has at least 10 tennis balls on her at all times.
